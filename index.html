AI: Artificial Intgelligence

AI is a field of computer science that focuses on creating systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence. This includes problem-solving, learning, and understanding natural language.

AI can be categorized into two main types: narrow AI, which is designed for specific tasks, and general AI, which aims to perform any intellectual task that a human can do.

Applications of AI include virtual assistants, recommendation systems, autonomous vehicles, and more.
